## üí° Tips for Company Outreach

1. **Use LinkedIn** to find specific engineers in applications or developer relations
2. **Start with Tier 1** - faster response, builds momentum
3. **For semiconductors**, look for "University Programs" or "Academic Relations"
4. **Reference specific products** you've used from that company
5. **For TI**, leverage your internship alumni status heavily
6. **Offer value first**: Position as helping their customers, not just asking
7. **Consider timing**: Avoid end of quarters (budget cycles)
